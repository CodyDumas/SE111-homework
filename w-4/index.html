Good Will Hunting

Good Will Hunting is a 1997 drama directed by Gus Van Sant. Will Hunting is a young janitor at MIT who turns out to be a secret mathematical genius. When his talent is discovered, he is ordered to see a therapist and work with a professor. The film is about Will learning to open up and figure out what he actually wants from his life.

Company Credits: Miramax Films, Be Gentlemen Limited Partnership

Release Date: December 5, 1997

Genres: Drama, Romance

Rating: R

Running Time: 126 minutes

Dead Poets Society

Dead Poets Society is a 1989 drama directed by Peter Weir. At a strict all-boys prep school in Vermont, a new English teacher named Mr. Keating encourages his students to think for themselves and embrace poetry. The film follows the students as they form a secret club and begin to question the world around them.

Company Credits: Touchstone Pictures, Silver Screen Partners IV

Release Date: June 2, 1989

Genres: Drama

Rating: PG

Running Time: 128 minutes
